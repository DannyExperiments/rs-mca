Read `COMMON_CONTEXT.md` first.

too-narrow literal pullback exclusion.

Prior external lanes suggested that denominators satisfying


`E_0(X^M)`. Punctured-fiber anchors may realize a full quotient-core profile,
not merely the smaller canonical quotient exponent.


4. Check official rates and source admissibility.
