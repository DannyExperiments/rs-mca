# ROLE 03 - Model Generalized-Jacobian Local-Limit Proof Lane

Append this role prompt after `COMMON_PROMPT.md`.

## Wall

```text
W-LIST-MODEL-GJ-QUOTIENT-CONDITIONED-LOCAL-LIMIT
```

## Objective

Work only with the model modulus

```text
Delta = [0] + sigma [infinity]
```

and a cyclic smooth domain

```text
H = alpha * mu_n.
```

Let `Q_per` be the quotient generated by characters whose pullbacks through
`alpha_Delta` are constant on cosets of a proper subgroup of `H`.

Prove a finite inequality of the form

```text
N_Delta(b)
<= N_Qper(pi b)/|ker pi|
 + C_sigma * binom(n,j)/|G_eff|
 + P_sigma(n)
```

with explicit integer `C_sigma` and `P_sigma`, or replace it with a sharper
correct finite statement.

## Constraints

- No asymptotic placeholders are allowed in the theorem statement.
- You may use Fourier analysis, character sums, or exact finite group algebra.
- The denominator must be `|G_eff|`, not naive `q^sigma`, unless you prove
  they are equal in the model.
- All periodic/quotient terms must be stated as exact finite objects.

## Success Criteria

Output `PROOF` if you prove a finite inequality usable by the certificate
checker.

## Failure Criteria

Output `COUNTERPACKET` if you construct a parameterized nonperiodic family
exceeding every stated residual bound after `G_eff` and `Q_per` conditioning.
